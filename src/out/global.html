changeView(session)

Sets the buttons, nav and titles according to the session status. If we initiate session we hide the button of the log and appears the nav and the button of the list of friends. If we close session disappears the nav and the button of the list of friends and the button of the login appears. Change the titles depending on whether we are logged in or not. If we are not logged in, we will empty the list of friends.
